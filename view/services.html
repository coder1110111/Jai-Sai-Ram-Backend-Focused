Our Services

At Jai Sai Ram Documentation, we offer comprehensive and hassle-free vehicle documentation services to simplify your post-purchase vehicle responsibilities. Here's what we specialize in:

1. New Vehicle Registration (RTO Registration)

If you've recently bought a new car or two-wheeler, our team helps you complete the mandatory registration process with the Regional Transport Office (RTO). We handle the paperwork, submission, and follow-ups to ensure your vehicle is legally registered and road-ready in Delhi or Haryana.

2. Fancy / VIP Number Booking

Want your vehicle to stand out? We offer special number booking services that let you choose a unique or lucky vehicle number, such as repeating digits or custom sequences. Our team guides you through the availability check, government fee structures, and booking process — making it easy to get the number you love.

Note: Fancy numbers are subject to availability and additional government charges.

3. RC Transfer (Ownership Transfer)

Buying or selling a second-hand vehicle? We take care of the Registration Certificate (RC) transfer process. This ensures the legal ownership is shifted to the buyer’s name smoothly — preventing any legal or tax issues in the future.

4. Assistance for Taxis and Commercial Vehicles

We don’t just serve personal vehicle owners — our services extend to commercial vehicles and taxis as well. Whether it's registration or fancy number booking, we ensure compliance with RTO requirements for business use.

5. Area Coverage

We proudly serve customers across Delhi and selected areas of Haryana. Whether you're an individual or a car dealership, we provide quick turnaround times and personalized support throughout the documentation process.
